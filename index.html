A little bit about us and what we have to offer:

This site is for hospital-bound children who can not take part in an in-person education system. Many times, children in hospitals are souronded by doctors and family membors who are focused on the childs health and not necessarily on their education. For this reason, we provide this system so that children and teens can learn and grow from their very own room. Here, there are classes available for numerous subjects some of which are math, english, science, history, language, and more. There are also extra carricular activities available! As you explore the dropdowns above, you will discover that there is a quiz which will help you recognize and unlock your hidden talent or intrest.
